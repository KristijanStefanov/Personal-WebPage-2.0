IznajmiLesno

IznajmiLesno is a modern, full-stack web application designed to revolutionize the car rental industry by enabling peer-to-peer car rentals between private individuals. Built using Next.js, Prisma, MongoDB, and Tailwind CSS, this platform allows car owners to list their vehicles for rent and renters to find and book cars directly from private owners, bypassing traditional rental agencies.
